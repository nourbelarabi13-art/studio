# **App Name**: Rosa Novara

## Core Features:

- Identity Sanctuary Authentication: A privacy-first sign-up using Firestore for secure account management, requiring only essential credentials and a strict age-verification gate.
- The Dark Archive Discovery Hub: An immersive homepage featuring masonry-style grids for featured novels, categorized genre browsing, and a performant search bar.
- Ethereal Novel Forge: A distraction-free writing environment for novelists with autosave drafting functionality and seamless publishing to the database.
- Immersive Sanctuary Reader: A comfortable reading layout optimized for emotional focus, featuring customizable typography and a minimal, focused reading view.
- Atmosphere AI Categorization Tool: An AI-powered tool that analyzes narrative tone and style to suggest optimal genre tags and ensure adherence to safety standards.
- Guardian Sentinel Reporting: A safety-focused report system integrated into every story, allowing users to flag toxic content directly to the moderator dashboard via Firestore.
- Personal Inkwell Vault: A secure section for authors to manage their drafts and published works, focusing on writer comfort and ease of revision.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Color: Velvet Garnet (#A3295C) — a saturated, vibrant hue used for storytelling actions and highlighting creative agency.
- Background Color: Obsidian Night (#171113) — a deeply desaturated and dark base that anchors the 'dark fantasy' aesthetic while ensuring readability.
- Accent Color: Ethereal Amethyst (#C270BD) — an analogous lilac shade that contrasts beautifully with the primary to distinguish highlights and active UI states.
- A pairing of 'Playfair' (serif) for evocative headlines and 'Alegreya' (serif) for the story body to ensure long-form reading comfort and an intellectual, literary feel.
- Fine-lined, minimalist vector icons with a touch of medieval ornamentation to match the gothic noir and dark fantasy atmosphere.
- A focused single-column reading experience with wide margins for air, and a balanced card-based grid for the content exploration hub.
- Atmospheric soft fades and delicate motion transitions (ease-in-out) that mimic the gentle turning of pages in a classic book.
